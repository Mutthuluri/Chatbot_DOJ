Welcome to the Department of Justice, India

The Department of Justice in India is responsible for the administration of justice, the implementation of constitutional mandates, and the development of policies for strengthening the legal and judicial systems. Our mission is to ensure the rule of law, provide access to justice for all citizens, and maintain an efficient and transparent judicial system.

The department oversees judicial reforms, manages legal aid programs, and collaborates with various state and central government agencies to uphold the rights of every citizen. Learn more about our divisions, recent news, and ongoing initiatives.
